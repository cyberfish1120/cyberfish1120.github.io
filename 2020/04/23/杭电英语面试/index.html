杭电英语面试常见问题：

你为什么选择杭电/为什么来杭州
First of all, Hangzhou Dianzi university has a high degree of computer science, with many famous professors. I hope I can make more progress in my major.second there are many development opportunities because the geographical position.then there is a  good study atmosphere with many internet company in Hangzhou, such as alibaba,xiaomi.

介绍一下你的家乡
My hometown is Hengshan, a beautiful city in Hunan Province. It is famous for its Hengshan Mountain. There are great scenes here. Each year, many people come here for tour and investment. If you have time, I can take you to climb the Hengshan Mountain. The scenery on the mountain is very beautiful.

介绍一下你的大学
I studied in Wuhan University of technology, a very beautiful school. There are lots of flowers and plants, and the air is very fresh. The teachers are very responsible and the students are energetic. I have learned a lot and have made a lot of progress. I am very grateful to my Almamater (iomemader), because it made me better.

你最喜欢的一门课是什么？为什么？
I like programming,so my favorite subject is data structure. Every language has its beauty,even computer language.Through studying data structure,you can know how computer work and you will say amazing when it solve the problem with differnt algorithm.

以后想成为什么，为此你需要怎么做？（研究生计划）
There must be many things to learn if I were enrolled into my ideal research field. I hope I can build up a systematic view of programming, especially in the fields of C plus.  On the other hand, I am dreaming of the participation of some relevant projects of my major, for this reason, I will get more experience in practice. In a word, I am looking forward to making up a solid foundation for future profession which is based on three years of study here.

​ Good morning,every teacher.I am glad to be here for this interview.

​ During my college years, I have worked very hard at my major courses, so as to lay a solid foundation of theoretical knowledge.
​ I was studying at home since I graduated in June 2019. During that time,I finish most of the C Plus programming at PAT.And I do the algorithm problem at leetcode now.I really enjoyed programming,and I hope I can concentrate on study and research in this field in my graduate time.
​ OK, that’s all.Thank you very much for your time and attention.

​ 各位老师早上好，很荣幸能参加贵校的面试。我叫ZZQ，今年23岁，毕业于武汉理工大学计算机科学与技术学院。
​ 在大学期间曾担任马克思主义研究协会技术部部长，任职期间主要从事对部员进行Android Studio、Adobe系列等生产力软件的教导。大二大三期间在学校学工办担任学生助理，主要从事协助各大办公事务处理和组织校内活动，曾参与举办2017、2018届校园招聘活动。
